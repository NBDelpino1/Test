The Story

Okay it's final project time and I'm solo. I would have prefered to work as a group but most people wanted to do their own thing. I thought to myself - you have 7 days to complete something AND show it off for demo day...what on earth are you going to do.

My instincts told me follow what you are most passionate about and don’t worry about the rest. That’s exactly what I did here. This is a recreation of the American Airlines website, as close to real deal as I could get in 7 days. I chose this site because from a visual perspective, it’s beautiful. The usability factor however is up for debate.

As with most projects, you have to make hard choices and I choose design over functionality. Sure I could have coded in some logic but that meant I wouldn’t have gotten the chance to really put my heart into what I loved most about coding - the design aspect. Honestly, I’m pretty proud of it - coming for no experience with coding to making this in HTML and CSS in such a short space of time. I mean it in the most humblest if ways... It’s a testament to how far you can go if you focus on the things you love. Hope you dig it.
